Billy J McBee – Official Biography (EPK)

Billy J McBee is a Malta-based songwriter and lyricist working in contemporary music development and independent songwriting. His work focuses on structured lyrical composition, melodic narrative writing, and adaptable songwriting across multiple commercial music formats.

He writes primarily in Maltese and English, and also works in Italian, with additional working familiarity in Spanish, French, German, and Bulgarian. This multilingual capability supports cross-market songwriting and European-oriented pop and commercial music production.

Musically, Billy.J operates across pop, rock, dance, R&B, and Euro-influenced commercial genres, with a focus on hook-driven structure, lyrical clarity, and adaptable song frameworks suitable for both independent and mainstream production contexts.
His portfolio includes original songwriting work and submissions to Maltese and European music initiatives, including submitted entries for national selection platforms such as Malta Song for Europe. He has also participated in local music competitions including Malta Summer Hit Song and other songwriting showcases, contributing original material for consideration within these formats.

His work has received online streaming exposure and informal radio play through independent releases and submissions, reflecting ongoing engagement with Malta’s evolving music and songwriting landscape.

Billy J McBee continues to develop his catalogue as an independent songwriter, focusing on cross-genre adaptability, collaborative development, and international songwriting potential.
